About this tool


The West Virginia Forest Carbon Estimator is a tool designed for landowners to determine the carbon credit potential of their forests.

Using state-of-the-art modeling and plot-based validation, the tool is currently the most accurate statewide estimator of forest carbon sequestration and stock The tool estimates the annual carbon sequestration potential of forested lands.

The tool was built by the Brzostek Lab at West Virginia University, in collaboration with the West Virginia University Bridge Initiative.
